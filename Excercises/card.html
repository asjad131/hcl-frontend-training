Bhimtal Lake

Bhimtal Lake is one of the largest Natural Lakes in India, located in the town of Bhimtal in the state of Uttarakhand. This lake is known to be the largest in terms of surface area in the Kumaon region and is a gorgeous sight for tourists. The island in the middle of the lake has been promoted as a major tourist attraction and is equipped with an aquarium showcasing the aquatic life of the region.

Chilika Lake

Chilika is a saltwater lagoon near Puri in Odisha. It is situated on the eastern coast of the country at the end of the Daya River. Chilika is not only the largest coastal lagoon of the country but also the second largest in the whole world. Many threatened species of animals and plants thrive in the lake and a large number of migratory birds visit the lake during winter as well.
